AboutMe.txt

Currently based in Washington DC.

First, I was into teeth, now I'm into code. Funny eh? Don't worry. I got the same looks when I told people I wanted to be a dentist, but majored in Exercise and Sport Science. After working for a year at an orthodontic office, I realized I was the tech savvy one that my co-workers depended on. Even in my college friend group, people would turn to me, not because I was the computer science major, rather because I would attempt to solve the problems first before seeking help. Those instances made me realize that I enjoyed the troubleshooting, the ingenuity, and deciphering the puzzles in technology.

Through coding, I tie in all my interests, creativity, and passion to turn what would have been a dream into reality.

Feel free to contact me with questions, suggestions, if you would like to collaborate, or just want to grab coffee.
